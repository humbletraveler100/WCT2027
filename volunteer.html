Why It Matters

This event only works because volunteers make it work.

01

Free, because of you

WCT 2027 is free for every attendee. Volunteer labor is what makes that possible — there's no ticket revenue covering the gap.

02

500 students, one day

Field Trip Day brings hundreds of Portland students to experience 15–25 cultures in a single afternoon. It runs entirely on volunteer escorts and coordinators.

03

A team that grows together

Many of our Team Leads started as single-shift General Volunteers the year before. There's room to grow into more responsibility over time.
